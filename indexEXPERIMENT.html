About Me

Hello! My name is Lauren McManamon.

I am a senior Computer Science major at Southern Methodist University's Lyle School of Engineering. The joy I get from my major and work stems from creating tools to simplify the user's life and bring beauty to the mundane; thus, front-end and user experience design are my specialities and where I desire my career to be centered around.

That is my current goal for acquiring a job, but I'm also here to prove that programmers are not binary beings (forgive the pun); I am a Renaissance woman. I believe that the key to success in any profession is to take the blinders off once and while and experience new, wonderful things. It's not only refreshing, but brings about new ways to solve issues you may had missed without that perspective. New vantage points are key to problem solving, and I surround myself with these kinds of people to encourage this ideal within myself.

The world of computer science is definitely a "boy's club" at times, and taking this leap has not always been simple.I attribute all of the blessings I have in my life to the unwavering support of my family and friends, without which I would be a fraction of who I am today.
